Adjust Parameters
CONFIG.filter_hp_cutoff
1.0 Hz
High-pass filter cutoff. Removes slow drifts. 1 Hz standard for sleep EEG.
CONFIG.filter_lp_cutoff
55 Hz
Low-pass filter cutoff. Removes high-frequency noise. 55 Hz captures Gamma band.
CONFIG.asr_stdcutoff
20 σ
ASR burst removal threshold (std devs). Lower = more aggressive cleaning. 20 is conservative for sleep data.
CONFIG.ICrej_thres
0.5
ICLabel rejection threshold. Components classified as artifact above this probability are removed.
CONFIG.rmchan_mincorr
0.60
Minimum channel correlation. Channels less correlated with their neighbors are removed as bad.
CONFIG.timefreq_window_len
5 sec
STFT window length for time-frequency spectrograms. Longer = better frequency resolution, worse time resolution.
