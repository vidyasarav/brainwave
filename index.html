§ MISSION
Our Mission

Neuroscience Belongs in Every Classroom

Founded in 2024, BRAINWAVE was built on a simple but powerful belief: when teachers understand the brain, students thrive. We bring cutting-edge neuroscience from UCLA's labs directly into K–12 classrooms—prioritizing under-resourced schools where this knowledge is needed most and where early exposure can change the trajectory of a child's life.

Our goal is not just to teach brain science—it's to spark a generation of curious, self-aware learners and to inspire future scientists from every community in Los Angeles.

§ MANIFESTO

The Science of Learning

We believe every teacher has the potential to be a catalyst for change. Through immersive workshops, hands-on activities, and exclusive tours of UCLA neuroscience labs, we equip teachers with the latest brain research — translated into tools they can use starting Monday morning.

LAUSD-Approved Professional Development JSPCC-approved workshops · Salary point credit for teachers

Teachers as Change Agents

Educators are the most powerful force in a child's scientific journey. We equip them with cutting-edge neuroscience knowledge, evidence-based pedagogy, and a community of forward-thinking peers — because when a teacher grows, every student in their classroom grows with them.

Equity at the Core

We prioritize under-resourced schools across Los Angeles where neuroscience education is rare but needed most. By emphasizing early exposure to brain science, we inspire students from all backgrounds to explore STEM fields and see themselves as future scientists.

Inside the Workshop

Teaching & Learning Experience

Each BRAINWAVE workshop is immersive, hands-on, and deeply collaborative. Teachers don't just hear about neuroscience — they experience it through interactive sessions, real research from UCLA labs, and peer learning that translates directly back to their classrooms on Monday morning.

01

Neuroscience Foundations

Active UCLA researchers share their discoveries directly — covering topics from memory and sleep to learning, development, and mental health. Science made accessible, relevant, and inspiring.

02

Classroom Applications

Every session bridges research to practice. Teachers leave with evidence-based strategies, lesson frameworks, and classroom-ready activities they can use with any grade level or subject.

03

Interactive Learning

From lab tours and brain specimen handling to VR demos and collaborative lesson design, BRAINWAVE turns learning about the brain into an unforgettable, multi-sensory experience.

§ FAQ
Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Q Do I need to pay to participate in a BRAINWAVE workshop?

No — BRAINWAVE workshops are completely free for K–12 teachers. BRAINWAVE is currently a volunteer-driven initiative. We are deeply committed to making these workshops accessible to all teachers and are actively working to expand support to sustain and grow this effort.

Q I am not part of LAUSD. Can I still join?

Yes! While we prioritize under-resourced Los Angeles schools, BRAINWAVE welcomes K–12 teachers from any public school district across California. All teachers who are passionate about neuroscience are encouraged to join — we strive to include as many educators as our capacity allows.

Q Do I need a science or neuroscience background to attend?

Absolutely not. BRAINWAVE is designed for educators of all subjects and grade levels — from kindergarten teachers to high school history educators. Our UCLA instructors are experts at making complex brain science accessible, relatable, and immediately applicable to any classroom. Your curiosity and passion for students is the only prerequisite.

Q Will I receive professional development credit?

Yes. BRAINWAVE workshops are JSPCC-approved by LAUSD and count toward salary point credit for eligible teachers. Participation certificates are provided to all attendees. We work with participating school districts to ensure the time you invest translates into recognized professional development.

Q What happens after the workshop — is there ongoing support?

The workshop is just the beginning. BRAINWAVE alumni join our Educator Community Network — a growing community of trained teachers connected through monthly UCLA webinars, peer collaboration, a curated library of classroom-ready neuroscience activities, and direct ongoing access to our UCLA researcher team.

Q How do I register, and when is the next workshop?

Submit your interest through our Teacher Interest Form — it takes less than 5 minutes. We'll notify you when the next workshop date is confirmed. Spots fill quickly, so joining the waitlist early is the best way to secure your place. Questions? Email vidyas@ucla.edu.

Still have questions? We'd love to hear from you.
