# Programs Overview

## Teacher Professional Development Workshops
Immersive neuroscience-based training for K–12 teachers across Los Angeles,
including LAUSD salary point credit workshops.

## Brain Awareness Week (BAW)
Annual large-scale community event engaging students in hands-on neuroscience learning.

## Summer Pathways Integration
Coordination with UCLA BRI undergraduate summer research programs serving
underrepresented minority students.

## Specialized Workshops
- Neuroscience of Learning and Memory
- Sleep and Brain Development
- Hands-on neuroanatomy labs
- EEG demonstrations
