# Mission and Vision

## Mission
BRAINWAVE bridges cutting-edge neuroscience research with K–12 education,
equipping teachers with evidence-based neuroscience knowledge and innovative
pedagogical strategies.

## Vision
To inspire early exposure to brain science—particularly in underrepresented and
low-resource communities—creating a wave of change in neuroscience literacy,
mental health awareness, and STEM participation.

## Core Values
- Scientific accuracy
- Inclusion and equity
- Early exposure to neuroscience
- Teacher empowerment
- Translational impact
